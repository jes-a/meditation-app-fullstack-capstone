insight•full

insight•full is a free and easy to use app that allows you to gain insights about your meditation practice. Simply add your session each time you meditate, along with any insights you had during your practice and save.
The app keeps track of your meditation sessions, and you can go back and read through your journal of insights.
Stay mindful, and stay insight•full!
